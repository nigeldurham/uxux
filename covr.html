covr

gig application

bringing traveling musicians and dynamic event spaces together

team

working alone, i researched, designed, and prototyped this product

ideation

deciding to focus on a niche market, i devised an application for musicians who perform in bars or small venues

wireframing

centering-in on a mobile-first, location-based platform, i sought to connect artists with nearby performance spaces - artists are able to create a profile, search for nearby event spaces, and book open performance slots

prototyping

running low-fidelity prototypes on marvel, i tested user flows for both businesses and artists. businesses check artist profiles and confirm bookings, while artists create a profile, share media, and message businesses in real time

usability testing

businesses noted that a multi-platform approach would be ideal for day-to-day booking, including tablet and mobile options. artists noted concerns about booking flexibility and venue-specific options for performers

feature prioritization

messaging and media interactions were prioritized for venue-matching, while artist-led requests were scaled back and social sharing was leveraged for on-the-fly events

final prototype

a multiplatform solution allowing artists and venues to share booking confirmation, payment, and performance information

product

a highly-interactive location-based cross-platform booking calendar and payment system for artists and venues
