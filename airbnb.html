airbnb

new feature

bringing custom events
to new spaces

team

working alongside 4 other designers, i developed the design system and branding for this experience. we shared roles iterating, testing, and presenting this product

ideation

with the goal of connecting users' events with event spaces, we designed a platform where users seeking to throw events could find a space to host their events

wireframing

seeking to bring together two personas (space-seller and event-thrower), we devised a portal where both parties can browse or search for available events or spaces

prototyping

in low-fidelity wireframes, venue owners are able to create a profile of their venue, include relevant venue information, and share openly on the platform. event hosts are similarly able to profile their event, include event details, and share publicly on platform. both parties are able to reach out to one another directly

usability testing

usability testing allowed us to identify primary user pain points, including issues with the general layout, some confusion about specific CTAs during task flows, and event tag-language that was uncommon or unhelpful

feature priortization

ample user testing and user interviews, along with relevant stakeholder considerations, funneled our core feature mods into a more cohesive product solution

final prototype

a functional user experience running on invision. usability testing confirmed that the flow was indistinguishable from the current airbnb application
