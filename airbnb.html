airbnb

new feature

bringing custom events
to new spaces

team

working with 4 other designers, we shared roles designing, testing, iterating, and presenting our product

ideation

we were posed the problem of designing a platform where users seeking to throw events could find a space to host their events

wireframing

seeking to bring together two personas (a "space seller" and an "event thrower"), we devised a unique portal where both parties can browse or search for available events or spaces

prototyping

running low-fidelity wireframes on marvel, venue owners are able to create a profile of their venue, include relevant information, and share openly on the platform. event hosts are similarly able to profile their event, include event details, and share publicly on platform. both parties are able to reach out to one another directly

usability testing

usability testing helped us to identify targets for modification within our flow, including minor variations in our existing information architecture for event details, general modifications to the user flow, and a streamlined overall journey

feature priortization

ample testing and user interviews, along with stakeholder considerations, funneled our core features into a more succinct product solution

final prototype

usability testing confirmed that the flow was indistinguishable from the current airbnb application - >90% approval rating across all users tested

product

a robust, detailed internal platform that integrates seamlessly within the airbnb user experience
