sunday

mobile gallery

a digital platform to buy and sell artwork

team

passion project: i executed all aspects of this product's design

ideation

i sought a way to connect
traveling art buyers or curators with local artists

development

i wireframed a flow for artists to be able to share their work locally or globally, while buyers are able to search for nearby or global artists

features

"dot/search" and "dot/about" (as seen on top middle and bottom right of screen) were devised to reduce icon clutter - these are introduced to the user in product onboarding

problem

while onboarding for the "dot/actions" went well, artist users expressed dismay at not being able to organize work across projects within the "instagram-style" upload flow

solution

researching artist archiving found on online galleries or personal portfolios, i implemented a structured information architecture for uploaded media that covered most common archive features

product

a dynamic mobile arts marketplace for a global or local network with flexible, relevant information architecture for archiving of artist work
