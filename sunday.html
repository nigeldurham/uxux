sunday

mobile gallery

digital platform to buy and sell artwork

team

passion project - personally executed all aspects of the design process, including user research, wire-framing, prototyping, and usability testing

ideation

local artists need a way of connecting with local curators or buyers. i sought to connect international, local, or traveling buyers with international, local, or traveling artists

wireframing

designing a flow for artists and buyers alike, artists are able to share their work locally or globally while buyers are able to search for nearby or global artists. buyers can approach artists via an internal messaging system accessed through the creator's personal portfolio

prototyping

artists are encouraged to upload media using their device camera, wherein they can include details about their work and choose to share on the platform. buyers are able to view shared work, learn about creators, and contact artists through the app

usability testing

artist were dismayed by not being able to organize work within or across projects, while curators noted they were seeking more detailed information about artist work, within or across projects

feature prioritization

researching archival features found on online galleries or artist portfolios, an archive system was designed for uploaded media covering most common archive features for fine artwork

final prototype

implementing structural changes in response to users' primary pain-points, the final product contains detailed information architecture inspired by fine art archiving

product

a dynamic mobile arts marketplace for nearby or global networks, incorporating flexible, relevant information architecture for the proper archiving of artist work in visually minimal personal portfolios
